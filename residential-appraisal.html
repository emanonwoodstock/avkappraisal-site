Residential Real Estate Appraisals in Sacramento

A residential real estate appraisal report is an independent, unbiased opinion that provides a certified estimate on the market value of a residential property from an accredited professional.

Qualified AVK Appraisal Services appraisers will determine the fair market value of your property providing an accurate estimate of what a home could sell for on the open market. We also protect buyers with details regarding its actual market value. Our licensed Sacramento appraisers offer estimates for a property's tax-assessed value.

Residential Valuations

A formal appraisal is essential for calculating a realistic listing price. Many sellers who have consulted with AVK Appraisal Services before putting their homes on the market have benefitted when selling real estate in Sacramento.

Appraisal for Home Buyers

Independent appraisals also give buyers a chance to verify whether the list price is reasonable. The lender will choose their appraiser for the loan and collateral valuation. The buyer is not the client of that appraiser. In some cases the buyer may want the additional security of their own appraisal; then they are the client.

Residential Appraisers for Tax Appeal

AVK Appraisal Services provides residential home appraisals for the purpose of determining accurate property taxes. Since many states base property taxes on the market value of your home; if your home in Sacramento has decreased in value, you may be eligible for a decrease in property tax based on the findings of an accredited appraiser.

Is an Appraisal the Same as a Home Inspection?

It is important to differentiate between a licensed appraisal and a home inspection. While a home inspection provides information on the structure and surrounding area of a residential property; an appraisal factors in additional pertinent information to arrive at a monetary value that is based on specific and qualified calculations made by a qualified and unbiased professional.
